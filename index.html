Vegetarian & Seafood Cuisine

Long Life Vegi House serves a large variety of delicious and healthy vegetarian meals, featuring tofu and several imitation "meats", such as vegi-chicken, vegi-beef, vegi-pork, vegi-duck, etc. These tasty healthy alternatives are based on soy protein (tofu, vegi-chicken, vegi-duck) and gluten (vegi-beef, vegi-pork). Many dishes feature plenty of fresh vegetables such as eggplant, brocolli, string beans, bok choy, cabbage and sugar peas . Both brown rice and white rice (as well as noodles) are available to complement the meals. Real Seafood (not vegetarian), including fish and prawns, is served. Fresh Ingredients

Long Life Vegi House prepares meals with plenty of fresh vegetables, and does not use MSG. Vegetarians rejoice! The imitation 'meats' are made of soy protein and gluten. If you're looking for affordable, high quality vegetarian cuisine, look no further. The seafood is real, it is not vegetarian.

Dine In or Take Out

The price of the vegetarian lunch special is $7.95 plus tax. The seafood lunch special is $8.95 plus tax. If you'd like chow mein or fried rice instead of steamed rice, add $1. The extensive and delicious dinner menu items are available anytime the restaurant is open. The reasonable prices really work for both small and large dinner parties. Order $40 or more (before tax), and get one order of Pots Stickers or Spring Rolls free!
